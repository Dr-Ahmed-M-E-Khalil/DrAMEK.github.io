Professional Profile

With over twelve years of experience spanning nanotechnology, sustainable materials, and chemical and environmental engineering, I am a distinguished academic and researcher known for pioneering sustainable technology and education initiatives. My career is highlighted by impactful contributions at prestigious institutions across the globe, including the University of Exeter, Kyushu University, and Cairo University, where I have advanced the frontiers of engineering education, research, and industry collaboration. Notably, my work has directed UK foundation industries towards a net-zero 2050, underpinned by a strong publication record and successful research grant acquisitions. As an advocate for the circular economy and sustainability, I am driven to develop novel technological solutions that address global environmental challenges, aiming to inspire and cultivate the next generation of engineering leaders.
